About Me

A research scientist and software engineer working on self-driving cars and connected vehicles.

I love everything about computer science and math.

My educational background and expertise are in Artificial Intelligence, vehicle communications, and machine learning.

Currently, I am part of the connected and automated vehicle group at Honda R&D Americas. My role includes research and development of perception-based applications for Autonomous Driving and Advanced Driver-Assistance Systems in the context of intelligent transportation systems.

I have hands-on experience in deep learning, data science, connected vehicles (V2x), and computer vision.
